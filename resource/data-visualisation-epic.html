The 12 Metrics I used to compare data visualisation tools

  • Graph animation, as sales people are really proud of animated power-point presentations.
  • Sales people love pie charts, are these supported in the tool?
  • Time-series graphs, as very important for monitoring
  • Zoom ability, to compare to a competitor of Hoptroff; and if granularity changes are supported without work
  • Which databases, and plugins are supported
  • GIS and mapping support
  • Rebranding via CSS
  • Work to reduce cost of creating dashboards
  • NLP support?
  • data projections?
  • support for alerting? and monitoring
  • list included maths features, to implement the above features, when they are absent
